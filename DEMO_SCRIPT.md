# 🎙️ VentPod Live Demo Walkthrough

**Duration Goal:** 60 – 90 seconds
**Tone:** Confident, clear, and unhurried. Let the UI animations breathe.

---

### 1. INTRO (5–10 seconds)
*(Start on the VentPod Landing Page or Home Screen)*
**🗣️ SAY:**
> "VentPod is an anonymous emotional platform where users can express themselves, connect through shared vibes, and communicate safely in real time."

---

### 2. CONFESSION FEED
*(Navigate to the Anonymous Feed. Slowly scroll through the glassmorphic cards.)*
* **Show:** A few anonymous posts, the reactions ("felt this", "same"), comments, and play a second of a music embed.*
**🗣️ SAY:**
> "Users can post raw thoughts anonymously, interact without identity, and even share music that reflects exactly how they feel."

---

### 3. VIBE MATCHING
*(Click over to the Vibe Selection Screen. Hover over the glowing cards and click one, like "Overthinking".)*
**🗣️ SAY:**
> "Instead of traditional random matching, users connect based entirely on their current emotional context."

---

### 4. CHAT SYSTEM
*(Wait for the match to connect and the chat UI to slide in. Send a regular, friendly message like "Hey, how are you holding up?")*
**🗣️ SAY:**
> "This enables real-time, meaningful, and strictly anonymous conversations with someone on the same wavelength."

---

### 5. AUTO DE-ESCALATION (IMPORTANT MOMENT!)
*(Pause briefly for effect. Type exactly: `"you are stupid"` and hit Send.)*
* **Show:** The message does NOT send. The De-escalation popup slides in over the input box.*
**🗣️ SAY:**
> "Instead of just blindly blocking harmful messages, our system actively intercepts them to help users rephrase and communicate better."
*(Optionally click "Send this instead" to show the safe message going through.)*

---

### 6. MUSIC IN CHAT
*(Copy a Spotify link and paste it into the chat input, or use the "Attach Song" button. Send it.)*
* **Show:** The Spotify iframe card loading inside the chat bubble.*
**🗣️ SAY:**
> "Users can also express complex emotions through music directly in the conversation."

---

### 7. SAFETY SYSTEM
*(Point to the glowing red Exit Chat button at the top of the interface.)*
**🗣️ SAY:**
> "From our mandatory upfront Terms popup to our instant emergency exit buttons, we prioritize user safety with clear boundaries."

---

### 8. CLOSING LINE
*(Click the Emergency Exit button, showing the safe redirect back to the home page.)*
**🗣️ SAY:**
> "VentPod is not just a chat app — it’s an emotionally intelligent platform designed for real, anonymous human connection. Thank you."
